about us

our story

Born, raised, and founded in the District. TasteLab serves as home to scratchDC, Tiny Grocery, and a few of our local friends who make some of the city's best food from our DC Commercial kitchen.

our mission

TasteLab is committed to creating a home and fostering a community where food businesses have the ability to grow, expand and prosper.
